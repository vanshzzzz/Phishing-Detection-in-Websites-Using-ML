Phishing is a type of cyber attack in which attackers try to steal sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, credit card numbers, or personal details by pretending to be a trusted organization or legitimate website. Usually, attackers send fake emails, messages, or links that look like they are from banks, social media platforms, or online services. When users click these links, they are redirected to fake websites that look almost identical to real ones, and when they enter their information, the attackers capture it.
The internet has become a necessary component of everyday life in the modern digital era for tasks like social networking, e-commerce, online banking, email correspondence, and digital payments. Although these developments have improved convenience, there is now a greater chance of cyberattacks. Phishing is one of the most common and harmful types of online fraud among the many cyberattacks.
Phishing websites are fraudulent websites that mimic legitimate and trusted websites in terms of design, layout, and URL structure. The objective is to deceive users into providing sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, credit card details, and personal information. Due to the increasing sophistication of phishing techniques, it has become extremely difficult for users to distinguish between genuine and fake websites.
Traditional phishing detection mechanisms are unable to detect newly created phishing websites and require frequent manual updates. Moreover, attackers continuously modify their strategies to bypass existing security systems. As a result, there is a strong need for an automated and intelligent phishing detection solution.
Machine Learning plays a vital role in addressing this problem by enabling systems to learn from historical data and identify hidden patterns associated with phishing websites. By analysing various features related to website URLs, domain information, and webpage content, machine learning models can accurately classify websites as phishing or legitimate This project focuses on developing a machine learning-based phishing website detection system that aims to improve online security, reduce cyber fraud, and protect users from malicious online activities.
